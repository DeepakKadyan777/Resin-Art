About Liquid Layers

Liquid Layers is a resin art brand that focuses on creating unique and high-quality resin products. Resin art typically involves pouring resin over various surfaces, such as wood, canvas, or other materials, and then adding pigments, dyes, or other embellishments to create stunning, glossy effects.

For Liquid Layers, the brand might offer a range of products, such as:

1. Art Pieces: Custom or pre-designed resin art pieces, like coasters, trays, and wall art.

2. DIY Kits: Resin art kits for enthusiasts who want to create their own pieces.

3. Supplies: Resin, pigments, and tools needed for resin art.
